# 🚀 Fabi Downloader

**Fabi Downloader** is a powerful and elegant Android application designed to download videos and audio from a wide variety of platforms with ease. Built with modern Android technologies, it provides a seamless and reliable downloading experience.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- **Multi-Platform Support**: Download content from your favorite social media and video sharing sites.
- **Historial de Biblioteca**: Visualiza tus descargas completadas y en progreso directamente en tu biblioteca con una interfaz fluida e intuitiva.
- **Background Downloads**: Keep your downloads running even when the app is in the background via a robust foreground service.
- **Real-time Progress**: Stay informed with detailed notifications showing download speed, progress percentage, and file size.
- **High-Quality Extraction**: Choose your preferred quality and format (Video/Audio).
- **Smart Storage**: Manage your downloads efficiently within the app and easily locate them on your device.
- **Advanced Options**: Custom support for cookies and expert `yt-dlp` arguments for power users.
- **Modern UI**: A clean, intuitive interface following Material Design 3 guidelines with support for Dark and Light themes.

---

## 🌐 Supported Platforms

The app supports a vast range of websites, including but not limited to:

- 📺 **YouTube** (Videos, Shorts, Playlists)
- 🎵 **YouTube Music**
- 📸 **Instagram** (Reels, Videos)
- 👥 **Facebook**
- 📱 **TikTok**
- 🐦 **Twitter / X**
- 🎮 **Twitch**
- 🟢 **Kick**
- ... and many more supported by `yt-dlp`.

---

## 🛠️ Built With

- **Kotlin**: The primary language for modern Android development.
- **Jetpack Compose**: For a fully declarative and responsive UI.
- **Material Design 3**: Ensuring a consistent and beautiful user experience.
- **Room Database**: For local persistence of download history and settings.
- **Coroutines & Flow**: Powering reactive and efficient asynchronous operations.
- **yt-dlp**: The core engine driving powerful media extraction.
- **OkHttp**: For reliable networking and API interactions.

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

Fabi Downloader is crafted with a focus on **simplicity** and **efficiency**. We believe that downloading content should be a "one-click" experience, without compromising on power or flexibility. The interface is designed to be accessible yet feature-rich, providing a professional feel to every interaction.
